✗ FAILS
combo-forestdo not use

Default wordmark

combo-copper on white or page-bg only. Primary mark in all branded contexts.

On dark surfaces

combo-navy on #1a1816–#302d2a only. Never use copper combo on dark.

Never recolor

Use only the provided SVG variants. No CSS fill, filter, or color overrides on any logo element.

No distortion

Preserve proportions. No stretching, skewing, rotation. Clearspace = icon height × 0.5 on all sides.

Antipatterns

Copper on navy or forest

Copper text or icon on navy or forest backgrounds — muddy, fails contrast.

Navy on ink or forest

Navy on ink disappears (near-identical darks). Navy on forest clashes.

combo-forest logo

Orange type on green background fails contrast. icon-green_type-orange.svg does not work — do not use under any circumstances.

Colored text on dark

On dark/ink surfaces: white or sand (#e1dcc8) text and icons only. Never copper, gold, navy, or forest as text color on dark.

Brand colors as backgrounds

Navy, forest, copper, gold are for buttons, badges, small accents only. Page and section backgrounds must be white or page-bg.

No generic tech palette

No cyan, purple-to-blue gradients, or neon-on-dark. These signal generic AI tooling — incompatible with Buchfink.

Brand Rules

Copper is the anchor

Every Buchfink surface references copper — logotype, primary CTA, or a key accent. It is the brand's warmth signal.

Warmth everywhere

All colors, surfaces, and type choices read warm. No cool grays. No sterile white-on-white. Every neutral leans warm.

Space Mono = data identity

Use for values, identifiers, labels, anything signalling precision. Never for long prose — that belongs to Rethink Sans.

Accent hierarchy

Copper = primary brand accent and default CTA. Gold = warning/premium, use sparingly. Never copper + gold as visual equals.

Surfaces stay warm

Page: #f5f2ed. Cards: #fff. Do not use pure white as the page background — it reads cold against the palette.

Dark mode: accents unchanged

In dark mode copper and gold remain the same hex values. Only surface and text tokens change. Shadows at 50% intensity.
